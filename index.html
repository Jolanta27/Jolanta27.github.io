My Formula For Success

Don't give up!

In the beginning, coding is hard. Continue to put in the work, celebrate the wins when you get them, and don't give up. It'll be worth in the end! Learn, test, strive to improve. You don’t fail unless you quit.

Be disciplined!

A highly skilled software engineer without discipline is like a veteran sailor without a map: really good at steering the boat, but can’t find shore.

Be comfortable with beeing uncomfortable!

You have to be working on things that are difficult for you. Even if you are distraction-free and getting a lot of coding hours in. If you’re just building the same really simple app over and over again you won't be successful.

Be A Team Player!

A good team worker is someone who is prepared to work on the project for the project. They are happy to not take the limelight and help others achieve they targets and objectives (as required) as they know that that in the long term will help benefit the company and team as a whole.
